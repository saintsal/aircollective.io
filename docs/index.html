What is Air Collective?

Air Collective a collaboration between many people and organisations. Our goal is to deliver breathing devices to everyone in need during the COVID crisis. We are completely non-profit and volunteer-run.

Air Collective focuses on fast-response countermeasures. Particularly, we recognise the exponential rate of virus spread, and the timeframe implications on overwhelming healthcare systems. We see that to be prepared as quickly as possible, we must work in the open, and enable locally-buildable devices. That strategy provides the fastest overall delivery times and are the most scalable production lines.

We support device-makers everywhere. We are an international team with roots in the UK, Nethlands and Bulgaria, and a professional network spanning Europe, Africa and Asia.
